# Fitness-Trainer
# Personal Fitness Trainer- AI #

## Overview ##
This project generates customized workout plans for users. By using Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Large Language Model(LLMS), the system will create a workout routine based on the users goals, current experience level, equipment available, and time available. The goal is to help people easily create workout plans that match their personal fitness goals and abilities

## Problem ##
Many people want to exercise and improve their health but struggle to create effective workout routines. Without knowledge of proper training methods or access to a personal trainer, it can be difficult to know which exercises to perform and how to structure a balanced workout plan.

## Proposed Method ##
The system will take user inputs such as fitness goal, experience level, available equipment, and workout time. These inputs will be sent to a LLM, which will generate a weekly workout plan with exercises, sets, and repetitions. Different prompts will be tested to improve the quality of the generated workouts.

## Data Sources ##
Workout information will come from publicly available fitness resources, such as online exercise databases, workout guides, and general fitness recommendations.
